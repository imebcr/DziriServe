Features:

24/7 Availability:


Our platform operates round the clock, ensuring that assistance is available whenever you need it. This means you can access services or post your availability as a worker at any hour, even in urgent situations and this point will help both workers and clients.

Empowering Young Workers:


Dziri Serve provides a platform for young, skilled individuals to kickstart their careers. They don't need to work for someone else; they can start their journey with us and showcase their skills to a wide audience "ya3ni menna w rayah mkch hadi ta3 mal9itch khadma!".

Flexible Work Hours for Workers:


We understand that everyone has their own schedules and commitments. Workers on our platform have the freedom to choose when they want to work. This flexibility allows them to balance their professional and personal lives.

Nationwide Coverage:


Our services extend to all parts of the country. No matter where you are in Algeria, Oran, Algiers, batna...and all the 58 wilayas! Dziri Serve is here to connect you with the skilled workers or services you need.

Empowering Communities:


Dziri Serve isn't just a platform; it's a community that believes in the power of connection and service excellence. When you join us, you become a part of this dynamic and supportive network.

Quality Assurance through Feedback:


We value transparency and trust. To maintain high service standards, we allow service seekers to provide feedback on the quality of service they received. This helps clients distinguish between skilled workers and those who may not meet their expectations.
